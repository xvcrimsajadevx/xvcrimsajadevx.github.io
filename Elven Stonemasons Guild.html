Elven Stonemasons Guild

Race: The Guild is run my the Mountain Elves, but any other alamia can join, as well as those of other races.

Country/Region: Their headquarters are on Vesta in Lenal, but they have Guild Houses in their Colony in Nyoryn also.

Lifespan: Depends on the race of the clan member.

Appearance: Normal appearance of Mountain Elves.

Personality Traits: That of Mountain Elves. Other races who join will adopt the quiet, calm countenance of a Mountain Elf as this temperament is ideal for stonemasons.

History: A simple history, one that dates back to the birth of the mountain elves themselves. This guild is responsible for the making of the Vallumglacia, Yaenara, the Ivory Tower of Yaquinta, the Fortress Nakra, and the bridges in Adenia, plus many more structures. They are organised, efficient, and always looking to make the best quality buildings and structures and better themselves.

Magic: Magic of Titan, earth majicks of human magic users can achieve poorer quality structures, but they still teach them their secrets.
