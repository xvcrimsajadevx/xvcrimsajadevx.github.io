Great Desert


Also called Urilian in the ancient tongue of the forgotten peoples of Eramor. Only the brave travel this road and for centuries the way stations on route have been abandoned. Since the humans abandoned the wilds of Eramor and the Elves retreated into their forests, merchants do not waste their time or risk their lives coming from/going to the north. Only the Tragorian Elves? ever used that route regularly, and only the brave continue to use it now.

Thousands of miles of desert and few inhabitants brave the heat and dry sands. The Trade Route goes through the desert and all the way to the southern coast, where docks exist that carry merchandise into the Lacanta Isles. Along this route are way stations that few still inhabit. Wizards keep the way stations stocked with food and pumps in the stone huts draw water for travellers.
