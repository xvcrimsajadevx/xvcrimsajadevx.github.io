Azure


Azure is one of the four Origin powers that created the first races of The Nether Realm. Azure is the Origin of water, the Goddess if you will, of water and the creator of the Azail Race.

Appearance


Azure is the crest of a wave, the whirling froth of the whirlpool, the currents and the depths of the oceans. She appears as a human form made and enshrouded in water, with fishes swirling around her. She can appear in any water form, even as an Azail, but most often she appears as a Siren. The Siren were made in her image, and sing the song of Azure, the song of the Seas...

History


Azure woke surrounded by nothingness, and filled with Light. She sensed her brother and sisters waking too and their powers as well as her own. Pondering her existence while she filled the first chasm with water, she tried to understand what is was she wanted from Creation. Covering the worlds in much needed water, breathing life into Titans creations, and giving Tempests the tears she so often wept, taking her tears into the great oceans the drops merging with her, becoming part of her, Tempests grief becoming her grief also.

Yet why did Azure weep? Was it for the empty waters she had made. Beautiful as they were, carving rivers through Titans strong mountains, crashing against the cliffs, the edge of his domain, it was not enough. She felt as empty as the vast oceans of All the worlds...

Deep underwater, in the darkest depths where Pyra's lights could not reach, Azure created life, the First of her races. the one's she called the Azail. She taught them the patience of the sea's, the wisdom of ages, and the love of all of Creation, the desire to see all the lands and the heavens, the love of Utinu and Tinu and the trapped Morwen... she gave them all the waters, the Gift of the Mother never to be forgotten.

Death ravaged All Worlds and Azure was stricken with grief. Overwhelmed by Tempests tears Azure swelled and enveloped All Worlds, unable to withstand the torrent or control it she created the Kraken. the Kraken took the Azail and protected them from Azures Grief. Even though her children did not die, they were bound to the waters forever. This grieved Azure more then anything and she hid herself in the depths of All Worlds... until she was called by the Light Bearers.

He made he promise, he enraged her and pleaded with her, and eventually he got his promise. She bound herself to the jewel he preferred to her. Invoking the Light within himself she saw the portion of his glory and bowed to it. She had forgotten her place amongst creation, in her grief she had forgotten her duty an an Origin. Returning the surface, but not to her people, she awaits the call of her Daughter, the Chosen One...

Majick



Azure's powers can only be used by her races. Each spells strength depends upon the ability and knowledge of the caster. When the spells can be cast depends on whether or not the caster is close enough to a water source. To make water appear out of seemingly thin air is impossible. Only the most powerful casters can draw water from very far away or take it from the atmosphere or from deep underground. I.e one cannot cast Cascade if one is in the middle of a desert. How these spells are cast also varies depending on the race of the caster. An Azail may control water differently to a Water Elf, and visa versa.

Spells include;

Blessing of Azure


The power to give the gift of underwater breathing to non water breathers.

Monsoon


The power to summon a flash flood

Whirlpool


Creates a whirlpool, multiple whirlpools can be created by powerful casters.

Tsunami


Summon the wave of the gods. Weaker magic users must be closer to the water.

Parting of the Waves


Caster parts the body of water so the sea/river/ocean bed is visible and beings can walk across the dry ground between the walls of water on either side.

Cascade


Causes torrential rain over a small target area. Can only be cast out in the open sea.

Typhoon


Summon a sea storm, must be near the shore but not in open water.

Water Wall


Raise an impenetrable wall of water.

Aqua Prism


Surrounds target with water and seals off air.

Aqua Sphere


Projectile ball of water that is destructive because of its speed. Upon impact it feels like one has been hit by a rock.

Aqua Torrent


Multiple streams of water controlled by user.

Draw


Draw water deep from underground rivers and streams, or from the atmosphere.

Divination


Find water sources that are hidden, cause springs to appear where there was none. eg; in a desert.

Song of the Siren


Hypnotic song that resonates in the ears of targets causing them to drown themselves in the depths of the waters.

Minuet of Azail


Raises objects from the depths. i.e City, sunken ship, treasures, etc.
