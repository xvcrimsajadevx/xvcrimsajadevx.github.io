Origins


The Origins are origins of the Elements. They were born of the Light, but are now independent of it, creating their own races and worlds. The Origins and their primary Races are:

Origin Name
Element
Primary Race
Tempest Air Dragons
Azure Water Azail
Titan Earth Elves
Pyra Fire Dwarves


The Origins appear in many forms, but more commonly as their pure element in humanoid form. Their races are imbued with the powers of their element, and can only cast spells that use that element. None of the Origins race can use the Ether. They are bound to their origin (except the Obsidian Elves who were cast off from Titan and have been imbued with the ability to use the Ether). This is known to the Elves as the Sundering.

Each of these races have majicks granted to them by the Origins. For a complete list of the Origin Majicks visit the individual Origin pages.
