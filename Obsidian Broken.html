Overview


Race: Obsidian Elf (No other allowed inside the clan)

Country/Region: The Dark Continent is their primary region. The headquarters is hidden deep within the shadows of the Onyx City. The clan has members throughout the whole Nether Realm, and is known and feared in human lands.

Lifespan: Obsidian Lifespan.


Appearance:


Either athletic, or brutal. The clan has a Black-red guild tabard, with two golden letters: OB on it. The Obsidian Broken are the elite warriors, but amongst this elite, will be found warriors more brutal, skilled, and cold blooded:

The Black Guard. The Black Guard wear a blood red-black armour and are armed with either: Dual Wielding blades/axes/maces, Wielding a monstrous two handed longsword/mace/axe and sometimes a spear. All these weapons are imbued with personally chosen magical capabilities. Their armour is capable of absorbing a limited amount of spells and hostile majick.

The Broken might name a Black Guard as a Bull. The name comes from what they are, these warriors do not stop, the continue until death devours them, or when their mission is complete. The Broken are fast, and deadly. They master both melee combat and necromancy. Click the link for an image.

They also have personal guardians for each battlemaster, 'customised' by the Battlemasters themselves. Some drawings are found, but they will not yet be revealed.


Personality Traits


Cold-Blooded?, Fierce, Sinister. Their loyalty against their leader cannot be broken. In addition to this, the member's own traits - usually Blacksmiths.


History:


A secret organization founded by Ysuran Blackfury a few decades ago. There is no exact date or time of the founding. These Broken seek to destroy the current Obsidian government, and cut their bond to the Darkness. These Obsidians want to regain the independence of their race. In these few months they have been planning on retaking Onyx City, slaughtering the heretics who do not accept melee combat or the guidance of the Light. And at this point it is revealed that these Obsidians also seek for the guidance of the Light, and are training on healing spells and Light Majick. They also honour Toah as the great Blacksmith.


Magic:


Necromancy and Death Majick, also the new art of Bladesinging: Channelling spells through cold steel on to their foe. Also, the greatest and most powerful enchantment the Obsidian are allowed to wield, is the Maelstrom Lighting. The lighting is a purple blast, launched from the caster, or channelled through the caster's weapon. The lighting burns it's target, and also drains the life force out of them. It is powerful, but can be very exhausting. Some of their most well-crafted weapons can wield up to Two enchantments.


Extra Info:


The Obsidian Broken have four Battlemasters, commanders of the Broken.

First Commander: Leader of the Blackguard, Durn Blackfury, son of Ysuran.

Second Commander: Master of Necromancy, Shin'Din Darkmist.

Third Commander: Died in the Battlefield. Master of Blacksmithing and Bladesinging. No Broken has proven themselves as capable of this task yet. The broken search for a new bladesinger.

Fourth Commander: Scouting and Assassination: Claws (Real name unknown)
