Overview


In the Nether Realm majick is the process through which the world is controlled, it is the foundation of Existence and the building blocks of the worlds. The Nether Realm is the only world where pure magic from the Light still exists. Everything comes from the Four Primal Powers, Light, Darkness, Origins and Ether. Each Primal Power is a different form of magic. Each one touches the world in a different way and each one is used by specific magic users. Not everyone can use magic, Humans are not always gifted with the talent, but all things are governed by it. Only 1/100 humes are born with the Ether ability.

The Darkness has its own races which have their own unique powers. They cannot use Origin magic, and only the Obsidian Elves can use the Ether. Dark Powers are often violent and consuming, earth shattering and chaotic. The world cannot handle the unnatural state of the Darkness and when dark powers are used the world suffers. The magic of the Light is rare and confined to a select few. The Light Bearer's and the clerics of the Light are the only one's who are actively using this power. The Origins magic has its roots deeply set in the Light, which is why Minions cannot (for the most part) use Origins powers. Light Powers are gentle, life giving and world building. They are in harmony with the worlds it created and touch it gently, keeping the balance and maintaining life.

The Origins each have created their own races, and each race has a different affinity and version of the origin spells. Unique in their own ways, they are tied to their Origin and to that element. The older and more developed the race, the more adept the spell casting. For instance, Dragons are the oldest race of Tempest and have the element of Air mastered for the most part. The Alatus however are still learning, and cannot control the weather, whereas Dragons can. Both are races of the Origin Tempest. Origin magic runs through the veins of the races they made. Origin races have inherent powers and the element flows through them, is a part of them, and they steer the element using every fibre of their body. It is intuitive, with learning comes a greater control and more powerful spells, but not all races seek to be a greater part of their element. The more the blood is mixed the less touch their is in the individual. Half races may have limited attributes when using the elements, as their blood is diluted. Origin magic flows, and moves with the individual, it cannot consume or take over the race that wields it as they are made from it and they can tap it with a thought and often without thinking.

Finally, the Ether is human magic for the most part (the Darkness has somehow forged Obsidian Elves with this power, and all are born sorcerers.). Humans are born with the ability to use the Ether, the final primal power. Some train as wizards, some as sorcerers, depending on their alignment, some train in various other disciplines like Shaman? or with Cults of the Djinns, and some never train at all. These are rogue magic users, some call them Mages. Mages can be very powerful even without training, but they tend to have a broader set of spells and cast differently to those trained. Their sspells are normally not as individually powerful as a trained majick user may be but they tend to have far more variation due to not specialising in one area of majick. A Mage could have elemental powers and a time spell they created, along with some illusion and healing, but a Wizard would only train in healing and a Shaman would be far more adept with time majicks. Those not trained in using the Ether often lose control of it, and never reach their full potential. Without training there are many high level spells they will never be able to cast.

As mentioned above their are some magic users who join groups or cults that teach them certain spells. The Cults of the Djinn's teach magic that only the Djinn they worship know. These people are as adept in magic as most wizards are.

The Ether manipulates the world and coerces it to do its will. Users of the Ether use this Primal Power as a cattle prod, nudging the world to conform to their will. Wizards use the idea of the Light and manipulate with hard work and control, whereas sorcerers tend to whip the world into submission and tear their path through to achieve their desires.

There are anomalies in majick, the Unknowns. These are magic users that have no apparent link to any of the Primal Powers. They are rare, and often linked to one of them somehow, but they keep the origins of their magic for the most part unknown, as well as their allegiance.

All the Origins Races can use the majick given to them by their origin. Races such as Dragons, Azail, Elves and Dwarves each have spells linked to their element.
