Omri

Affinity: Nature

Appearance:

Normally uses whatever is around him to make his form, leaves, vines, stone. In any form he always has green, glowing eyes, that mirror his beloved. Sometimes though, he is just a young man, very elf like, roaming the woods, seemingly aimlessly.

Temples:

He desires no worship, but still has a few temples built by loyal followers, his Druids. The few temples he has were never built, but cultivated. They are groves of trees, with a stone altar in the centre, weathered and covered in vines. His druids are beloved by him and although he does not need them to worship he enjoys teaching them about nature and cultivating their magic's.

Powers:

Omri's powers are rooted in nature. He inspires growth and gives life. Breathing essence into withered trees and dead flowers, giving the Nether Realm its grieves and gardens. The great forests of the Nether Realm are protected by Omri. His anger is only incurred by those who mistreat nature and abuse it. He has a great hate for the races of Darkness, particularly the Obsidian Elves.

Extra Info:

Omri is the lover of Tailtiu and resides always with his Druids when not with his faery Queen.
