Slavers of the Western Sea's

ALL IMAGES IN THIS ARTICLE NOT ORIGINAL TO TNR

GROUP/GUILD/CLAN/TRIBE NAME: Slavers of the Western Sea's (in the guild of slavers that unites all the slavers in the world under the same banner and forces them into the universal conditions of the Slave Trade.

RACE: Hume, mostly

COUNTRY/REGION: Western Sea's; their hideaway is on a deserted island in the deep ocean in the far west.

LIFESPAN: Same as their race.

APPEARANCE: The appearance of the individual slavers vary depending on race. they have the look of pirates for the most part and all have a tattoo of their slaver flag (in this groups case all their ships have a flag that is a black heart in white chains) in their hand.

The main ship in the fleet is called the Cutlass and the Captain is Ganray Zanziba, a ruthless and notorious slaver who trades between the Necra and Sedanian's on a regular basis. The Cutless is a Clipper Ship whose design is traditionally used for merchant ships which have huge cargo area's. Slaver vessels are all normally cargo ships, their cargo mostly slaves of course.







Many of the ships in this fleet are in disrepair, but they are functional and deadly.

PERSONALITY TRAITS: these slavers are ruthless, amoral, cruel... in many cases considered worse then pirates as their penchant and need to hunt people to sell as slaves sets them aside from the average pirate. Pirates may kill and take ships and cargo but for many slaves they are doomed to a fate worse then death,. Many of the crew on any of this fleets slaver vessels will rape the women and torture the men, tossing an new born babies over board as little money can be got for a babe.

HISTORY: Originally they came from Sedana and still have connections there. This particular group are part of a world wide trade that have slaver docks in the Isles de Mortis, Sedana, Eraosia, Aeternus Caliga, The Forgotten Isles and other random places. This fleet do the slave run between the Isles de Mortis and Sedana on a regular basis, having places to dock for the winter in Sedana and places to lay low in the Isles de Mortis.

MAGIC: They have anti magic items made in Sedana and some of their fleet specialise in capturing majick users for the use of Sedana in making these anti majick items. Some ships employ mages and sorcerers to aid them as well.

Miscellaneous Facts: The number of ships in this slaver fleet are unknown. Those that are known are as follows;

  • Cora - a Clipper, crew of 100.
  • Lubeck - Cargo ship, crew of 200
  • Salamander - Caravel, crew of 75



A light two-masted lateen-rigged vessel, and because of its smaller size the Salamander is able to explore upriver in shallow coastal waters. With the lateen sails affixed, it is able to go speedily over shallow water and take deep wind. As the caravel is very fast it is able to outrun most ships.


  • Tecora - another caravel, crew of 100
  • Wanderer - -schooner, crew of 40



The Wanderer is used to carry cargo in many different environments, from ocean voyages to coastal runs and on large inland bodies of water.


  • Wildfire - another schooner, crew of 50
  • Zong - Clipper, crew of 200

These slavers are one fleet of many slavers in the world and are typical of the set up of the slavers. The slave trade is rife and fairly organised, they are similar to pirates but far far worse.
