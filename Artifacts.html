Artifacts

Artifacts have existed since the creation of time, since the first being of the first race took their first breath. Some artifacts have a rich and detailed history, becoming known across the world as an artifact through centuries and centuries of storytelling. Others were created for a specific purpose, becoming and artifact because of what it was made to do. There are many artifacts scattered throughout the world and many more exist than are known about. The list of known artifacts is a big one, and so we have broken things up to make it easier for you to find.
