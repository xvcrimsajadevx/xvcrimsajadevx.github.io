Overview



Name: Sephya


Location: In the southernmost area of the Ice Continent is a land that belies the biting cold and frosty winds.


Major towns, cities or landmarks:

The Ice Oracle has a temple located here and is the only part of the land that has ice. The temple itself is made of ice.



Synopsis of Country/Region


The land of Light is sustained by the ever shining sun, Morwen. She drives away the cold and allows the grass to grow and plants to flourish under her warm light. Darkness has never touched this land, and the Ever Shining Sun bestows life upon this small patch of green in the midst of white. Encircling mountains surround this land which is 30 miles long and 20 wide. Sephya is unusual as the sun, Morwen, only hangs above Sephya and sheds light upon her. Where darkness falls outside of the encircling mountains Morwen can be seen, the southern light in the distance floating over Sephya.


Geo-political Infrastructure


The Ice Oracles Hollow is the centre of intelligent life in this land. Her temple is the only structure and there are no settlements here as the land is deemed sacred. A haven of light and life no one dares to spoil her land. If there is a ruler, it is the Ice Oracle, but most would see her more as a Keeper, and caretaker, and overseer.


Religion and Culture


None.


Laws of the Land


Only one may enter the inner sanctum of the ice oracle.
