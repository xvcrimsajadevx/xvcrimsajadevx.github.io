Shalimdor and Kalimdor


Shalimdor (Quiet Lands)

Located in the north west of the Eastern Continent, the Quiet Lands are peaceful and the only area of the northern region of this continent with a coastline of beaches. The land ends at the foothills of the Umikabi Mountains, the mountain range cutting off these plains from the rest of the land. There is no way to the other side of the mountains as Umikabi has no mountain passes. The Druids of Omri have dwelt in these lands for millennia, its peace and tranquillity perfect for their meditation and the forests the homes of their people.



Kalimdor (Calm Lands)

With Terra to the north and east, The Rift to the south and east and Umikabi west the Calm Lands are surprising calm and peaceful. Hundreds of miles of plains and flat lands Kalimdor is a land of Titan's smaller races and more peaceful creatures. Nymphs?, Unicorns? and Faeries? live peacefully here.
