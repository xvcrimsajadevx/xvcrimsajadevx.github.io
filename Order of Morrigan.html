Order of Morrigan


RACE: Only humans see the Djinn as gods, therefore only humans would worship Morrigan in this way. It is rare for members of other races to join this group, but it is not impossible. As Morrigan is the Djinn of war there are more members of her cult from other races then the average Djinn.

COUNTRY/REGION: The Order are found mainly in the Temples of Morrigan, but they do have safe houses around the world from which they recruit people. The temples are all over the world, and known by most people. There is only one that is hidden and only the highest members of the Order know its location as it is where Morrigan dwells.

LIFESPAN: Depends on the race and magic of the Cult Member.

APPEARANCE:


Her Priestesses mimic her appearance as dark maidens with raven hair and blood red garments. All Priestesses look similar, their race being the only definitive differences. They all dress in black hooded cloaks and red slashed dresses, their pale skin exposed. Some mistake them for Vampires?. The Knights dress in Crimson armour, always. The Maidens in simple black and red dresses and the Disciples in black garments.


PERSONALITY TRAITS:

The Priestesses are strict, tough and sometimes rather arrogant. They are powerful beings and often use their magic to get their point across. The Knights are obedient, subservient and loyal to the Order. The Maidens and the Disciples are always trying to look good in front of those ranked above them, in the hope that one day they may dwell among their god also and receive those divine gifts.

HISTORY:

Morrigan has always been and has always welcomed worship. She chose her first Priestess from amongst the female warriors of Eraosia and has chosen many from that country since. She is worshipped as much as Uphaz in that country, but Teran's and Adenian's also share this worship. War appeals to many and it does you good to have the Djinn of War on your side.

For centuries the Order has been open and never changed. The last 50 years though Morrigan has not allowed any new members into her Order and the Priestesses have disappeared. The Knights have not been seen in battle for 145 years and the temples have shut their gates. None go in or out of the Black Temples of Morrigan and Avalon? is starting to forget their ever was a Djinn of War.

MAGIC:

They can have premonitions of a warrior's violent death, they can command and summon Banshee's and can influence the tide of war. Their touch can imbue people with fury and the battle fever. Unlike their goddess they are not warriors. they are protected by the Knights though, as they are still mortal.



EXTRA INFO:

To rise through the order Morrigan has to choose you personally. Those elevated to High Priestess or Acolyte have to endure harsh tests, some people die during this initiation. Strength is valued and tested all the time.


Any who are interested, Morrigan needs priests and priestesses to run her Order. Right now ALL SPOTS ARE OPEN. If you would like to apply, just post the app like normal and pb and I will approve it from there! Thanks!

Keep in mind that even though they are old they are still young appearing and their bodies are young as well since time passes differently in Morrigan's temples.


Primate Oldest, highest member of the Order of Morrigan. (very old)
--There is only one Primate for all of Morrigan's temples who prevails over them all.

Prelate (Female) Patriarch (Male) Prelate is the High Priestess while Patriarch is High Priest

Exarch Prevails over the training for all the Priests and Priestesses below him or her.

Metropolitan Prevails over the upkeep/food stuffs/and majickal inventory of the temples and inhabitants.

There is one of each Prelate, Patriarch, Exarch and Metropolitan for each Temple.



All of the below positions tend to the discipline, worship, and the training of the acolytes, porters, and lector's.

Archbishop (one per temple)
Bishop (two per temple)
Archpriest (one per temple)
Priests and priestesses (12 per temple)
Lector (one per temple) the one who reads important documents of Morrigan, or teachings, etc
Porter (one per temple) deals with the music in the temple, usually whatever the higher ups prefer
Acolyte - Brand new students in the Order of Morrigan

Acolyte's do not have to ascend through the temple one step at a time, usually they skip porter or lector rank, it only depends on the archpriest's decision. It can take a very long time to become an arhcpriest or higher. Their positions can be challenged as this is a temple of war. Anyone can challenge anyone's position except for the Prelate, Patriarch, Exarch, or Metropolitan. These can be challenged but only directly through Morrigan. She requires to oversee these sorts of challenges and must approve the challenge.



Further Information regarding other roles in the Order:


Hand Maiden's of the Temple : These women are being trained as High Priestesses. Unlike those they serve they retain the features they were born with. There are always three Maidens to each High Priestess.

Crimson Knights of Morrigan : Male and Female, any age, any race, these Knights are Morrigan's personal army. They are changed into a sub-race of human and serve only the War Djinn. They are drawn to her battle horn and appear on any battle field when she calls them. The numbers of these Knights are not known, but some speculate that it is possibly around 5,000

Disciples of Morrigan : These are the lowest ranked of the Order and the bulk members of the cult. They do not have any gifts bestowed upon them.
