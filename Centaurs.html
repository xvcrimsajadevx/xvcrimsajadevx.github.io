Overview


Country/Region: North of the Eastern Continent?, in the land of Terra.

Lifespan: They live to around 400 years old.

Appearance:




The centaurs are creatures that are part human and part horse. They have the torso and head of a human, and the body of a horse. The human part of them is that of a topless man or woman. They have no shame when it comes to this kind of nudity, and embrace the nature of their form. Blonde, dark, red headed male and female centaurs propagate Terra, their horse part often reflecting and complimenting their human parts. A blonde headed blue eyed Centaur would often have a white or beige horse flesh, and a blonde tail, for example.


Personality Traits:

The personalities of Centaurs varies. Some are mystic and reflective, pondering the wonders of the Universe and tending nature. Others are archers, hunters and warriors, depending on their mood, the state of the world and those who enter their land. They are a curious race, but a very un-trusting one too. They love the earth and their connection to it and Titan keeps them protected and keeps them unified.



History

[+]

The tales of the Centaurs are told only by wizards, as this race will not suffer any other type of human or race to enter their land, Terra. The only other exception to this rule are the Unicorns?. They are believed to be the most divine of creatures and Centaurs will protect them with their lives. Unicorn dales in Terra are home to the most numerous wild herds of Unicorns? in the entire Nether Realm?.



Most notable Centaurs


Ciern


Ciern was known for his exceptional goodness and wisdom. He was the first Centaur to leave Terra and he became the tutor of earth magic on the Wizarding Isles. He was a master of the stars and the heavens and taught early wizards the secrets of his people. In Terra his name is blotted out, but wizards are allowed to enter Terra because of this act. They are seen as cousins and are respected by the Centaurs.


Euryt


Led the Centaurs in the battle against the dark wolves. He went as far as Hazar Shual?'s sacred valley, believing the Djinn to be the maker of the minions. Euryt was taken prisoner by the Djinn, and returned to his warriors seven days later. He led them back to Terra but never spoke of his meeting with Hazar Shual?. Centuries later, an aged Euryt was called to battle by the Djinn. They fought side by side against the dark hounds, but Euryt fell in battle. Even to this day young Centaurs offer their bow and blade to Hazar Shual?.


Ixion


The current leader of the Centaurs. Every Centaur is subject to him, but he does not elevate himself as a king or emperor. He is merely the representative of his race to others.

Terra is a land where nature flourishes. Its beauty is marvelled by all who come upon it, and worshipped by the Centaurs who tend it. They have but one city, te'Knossos. The city itself is in the centre of a labyrinth which only the chosen can navigate.


Majick


Centaurs use earth magic to understand the workings of the universe. They are great astronomers and can predict the fate of others by searching the stars. Some call them Mystics, they call those who are the most proficient in this area; Seers.


Centaur Herds


The herds are divided according to the land they inhabit in Terra. The Knoss Herd being the most prominent as all their leaders have come from that Herd.
