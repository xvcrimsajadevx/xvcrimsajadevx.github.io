Overview


Name: Atra Arcis and other Aluka forts

Location: The Aluka have fortresses all over the land, all but one on the coasts. The Atra Arcis is in the centre of the land, all roads lead to here.


Major towns, cities or landmarks:


Capital- Atra Arcis


Aluka? forts or vaguely reminiscent of hume constructs, but that is where the comparison ends. Every single fort is a carbon copy of the first, Atra Arcis. Black stone walls and buildings, black iron gates and portcullis, upon the black scorched ground of the Aeternus Caliga?. These forts line the coasts and stop anything getting in or out that is not Aluka. The Aluka? are the dominant sub-race of the lands of the Undead and they keep it. The fort is simple inside and outside. It is busy, full of barracks and has one building in its centre that is the Razna, the meeting place of the Aluka Generals.


Synopsis of Country/Region:


The great northern highway leads from here to Ramakasiz and along this road there are gargoyles every twenty paces. Anything not Vampire? is turned to stone/into vampyres upon this road, and this road is the only known way to enter either City/Fortress. The land off the road is plagued and blighted, anything not Undead could not survive for long in it, these are the Wastes of Zircun which surround the road and the forts and the city of the Lamia, and make up the rest of the Blighted Continent. The forts of the Aluka are beyond formidable. They have never been breached, and wizards and dwarves have tried.


Geo-political Infrastructure:


The Aluka? Generals run these forts, one in each fort along the coast. The Generals answer to an unknown Thyrza?.

Laws of the Land:



Aluka laws are simple. The Aluka are in charge. Do not disobey an Aluka?, even if you are a Lamia? noble or Osiri? himself. These forts are the Aluka's and when you enter them you are at their mercy no matters who you are.
