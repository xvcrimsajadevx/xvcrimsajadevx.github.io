Overview


Clerics are individuals who get powers from their god. They are imbued with abilities they may not have been born with (so for instance a hume could worship a Djinn and that Djinn could give them the Ether ability). Cleric powers are usually prayed for and a cleric must have faith to use their powers. A deity will know if their cleric has lost faith and will withdraw their powers.


Types of Clerics


Clerics of the Light


Some believe they are myth, lost when the realm of Gallian was destroyed. These clerics are the first type of cleric and the most powerful. There are still churches dedicated to the Light all over the world and some of these churches have genuine Clerics of the Light leading them.

Cleric of the Light powers may include;

    • Life Giver - this is a power to breath life into organisms, such as plants, animals and sometimes even humes.

    • Self Regeneration (only clerics trained in Yaquinta have this ability)

    • Heal Minor Wounds (self and others)

    • Heal major wounds (self and others)

    • Open Minds - this ability is reading the minds of others using the Light to see the true intentions of people.

    • Enlightenment - a spell that helps a person see their true path or remember something lost and long forgotten.

    • Bless - reverses the effects of any curse performed by a Minion.

    • Sanctuary - can transport a cleric to the closest church of the Light, and for senior clerics they can choose their location


Origin Clerics


Sometimes referred as Priests or Priestesses, these are individuals from the origins races who specialise in specific spells granted to them by their origin. (See the bestiary for additional Origin Races beyond their primary races).

Powers of origin clerics are normally mastered versions of the Origin spells and race spells, but for clerics they can only perform them if they have faith and the stronger their faith the more powerful the spell. These clerics also have the added ability of being able to summon the avatar of their origin.

Djinn Clerics


Sometimes referred as Priests or Priestesses, these are individuals from the Djinn races or those imbued with powers by the Djinn who specialise in specific spells granted to them by their God/Goddess. Djinn Worshippers are not uncommon, but the clerics or priests/priestesses are. These individuals are normally high up in the cult and are granted specific majicks and powers by their god/goddess.

Djinn clerics can also call upon their deity and summon them in times of need.
