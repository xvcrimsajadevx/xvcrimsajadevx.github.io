House Ravencloud

NOBLE HOUSE NAME: Ravencloud

RACE: Human

COUNTRY/REGION: Sedana

SIGIL/COAT OF ARMS: Raven

PERSONALITY TRAITS: n/a

HISTORY:

Mika Ravencloud is an ex-Paladin and was raised as Sovereign from a Noble House. Not an Aristocratic House of Sedana, but a Lower Noble House, one that centuries ago made its own name by making it own money in the Gold Rush. House Ravencloud has steadily grown in stature, it's Manor house one of the most beautiful and the countryside surrounding Edana. The capital of Sedana is where the ancestors of Ravencloud hail from, and the Mountains behind it where Ravencloud had its first mines. Still a rich house in Sedana Ravencloud tend to send their eldest Son's to serve the Paladins, hoping that one day they will be raised in that Order. Ravencloud believes it owes its Wealth and Fortune to the God of Sedana, to Zadok, and ever member is a faithful follower of Zadok and respect and aid the Priests and Acolytes where they can.
