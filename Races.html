Original Races


Alatus

A winged race of Tempest, an angelic looking race that are the Air Origin's second born after the dragons. They live on the floating city state of Volatilis.

Ny'eki


Sub-race of the Alatus

Azail

An underwater race and firstborn of the Origin Azure. The Azail live mostly underwater but have cities in and upon the great oceans and some venture onto the land as well.

Centaurs

The Centaurs are part human, part horse and reside upon Terra in the Eastern Continent. They are a bestial and private race and

Djinn

The self proclaimed gods of this world, a race born of the Ether itself, and all powerful.

Dragons

The First race of Tempest and the oldest race of the Nether Realm. Nado is their King and the traitor Tiamat their Queen.

Dwarves

The First race of Pyra and the Lords of Fire. They dwell in the Mountain of Fire and mine the ranges of the Nether Realm, specializing in magical weapons and armour.

ech'Lun

A race of biped wolves who were made by the Djinn Hazar-Shual. Some call them Lychans, but they cannot shift their form and are so much more then wolves. They are the Lords of Malikar and the Jungles of Rathrabin, and serve only Hazar-Shual and his Shual wolves.

Elves

The first race of Titan, the Earth Origin. The elves of the Nether Realm abide for the most part on Lenal, which they call Elvenhome and are divided into six sub race of elf (alamia). Water, Ice, Winged, Forest, Mountain and Obsidian.

Eria

The race who are the shadows of the Djinn and reside in the Shadow Realm. They are the summons of the Nether Realm and are the gods of the Quasi and the Vigilia.

Half Races


Humanis Draconis

A race made by the Eria Zaci in the Shadow Realm, and forced to exist in the Nether Realm. They are humanoid dragons who can shift into the dragonlike form of draconis.

Humans

Humes, some races call them. Like you and I, the humes of the Nether Realm are many and all over the world.

Barbarians


Sub race of hume species

Halflings


Sub race of hume species

Quasi


Sub race of hume, can summon the Eria.

Summoners


Sub race of hume, can summon creatures from the Shadow Realm but cannot summon the Eria and do not have the extended lifespan of a majick user.

Magi

Elementals, pure energy, pure elements. The Magi are a race made by no one, they are an evolution of majickal humes who break the laws of nature and are punished by taking the form of the element they abuse.

Necra

A humanoid race made by Apollyon. They are death worshippers and live on the Isles de Mortis. They are cousins of the Vigilia.

Oracles

Made by the Djinn, the oracles are a race of Seers who live in Hollows scattered throughout the realm.

Shifters

As their name suggests they can shift into any form. No one knows their origins and their majicks are not of this world...

Undead

Vampyre, zombie, lich. All those who were created by the Darkness are undead races.

Vigilia

A humanoid race made by the Eria Ta'Ko and Moriqueeta. They are the protectors of the Quasi Summoning Tribes and are bound to the summoners fate.



Member Created Races


Avian

Another winged race of Tempest, the Avian are part bird part hume.

Cael Avien

The third Winged Race of Tempest, this race though are most bird-like in their looks and society of all of Tempests races.

Kikumin

Humanoin race, created by Ironside. They reside in the City of Tyaka, Mount Inioko, Eastern Continent, a location created by Ironside.

Suna'arashi

A desert race of Titan, created by Ra'ha'dek. They are a humanoid race who can control sand. They live in the Suna'arashi Empire in the Great Desert.

Zekten

An abomination, a twisted race of Titan who dwell upon Lenal and are the elves Nemesis.
