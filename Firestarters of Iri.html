Firestarters of Iri


RACE: Human

COUNTRY/REGION: Mainly Eraosia, Fenlar and Adenia

LIFESPAN: Depends on their ability with magic.

APPEARANCE:

The Firestarter's are proud cult members and all dress in similar black slacks edged in red flames. They tend not to wear tops and almost all members are males, any females are restricted to the temples of Iri (for Iri's pleasure as well as the upper members of the group.) Their tops are always black shirts, with leather boots on their feet, and leather armlets. The armlets have no real use, except perhaps to identify a member dressed in civilian clothes. All male members have long hair and dye their hair black to create further collectivity.

PERSONALITY TRAITS:

Chaotic men who often get consumed by their element they are always men who are magic user's. The Firestarter's of Iri are a popular cult and those boys who find themselves developing magical powers in puberty often run away to join Iri. they are violent and treat people contemptuously, taking and never asking, be it food, homes or women. The Djinn Iri is their god and they worship him fiercely.

HISTORY:

The History of this cult goes back millennia, and starts with Iri's betrayal of Adonii. The two Djinn were lovers and roamed the Nether Realm together. When Iri lusted after a human a war that has lasted for thousands of years erupted between the two Djinn. Iri is merely defending himself, or so he would say, yet over the years he has developed a lust for power. He envies Zadok, Uphaz and Eleph and seeks the power over countries that they wield. His cult worship him in temples all over Avalon? and he gains power with every human who joins him. Not all members are magical, indeed he recruits all kinds of people to aid him in his cause, his dominance over Avalon? his primary goal.

MAGIC:

Most members of Iri's Firestarters are pyromaniac's, whether they can use magic or not. Arson and fire magics are used as weapons against their enemies.
