Overview


Name: The Wastes of Zircun

Location: These are the swamps and canyons to the east where the Lamia commoners live and where the slave camps of humans are.

Major towns, cities or landmarks:


The wastes stretch for hundreds of miles and are the hovels and mines of the common Lamia?. There are holes in the black earth that spew molten lava and stench of gases make the ground uninhabitable for humes. The main mine sites are dotted all over the wastes, joined by small roads that run along side the tracks of the carts that transport the food to and from Ramakaziz. Underground the hume farms and mines a dank, dark and hot places that are reminiscent of the demon realms, such is the hell for all who exist there.


Synopsis of Country/Region:


Simply, it functions as somewhere to keep the rest of the Lamia? population, and waste away in uncivilised and savage society, and somewhere to harvest and grow their humes, to drain their blood and send it to the nobles in Ramakaziz?.


Geo-political Infrastructure:


There is no structure. The only thing that is remotely organised are the hume farms, which are overseen by the half bloods cast out of Aluka? and Lamia? society.
