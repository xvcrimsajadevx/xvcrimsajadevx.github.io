Elysia

Appearance:

Elysia appears in three forms when summoned, representing the three aspects of the Existence.
As the young child she is enchantment, inception, expansion, youth, excitement, and a carefree exotic aura. She is always dressed in white in this aspect.

As a maiden she brings life, rebirth, healing... she is always dressed in red in this aspect.

And as an old crone she gives wisdom, casting spells never seen before. She is always dressed in black in this aspect.

Its depends on the phases of the moon as to which one she is summoned as.

Powers:

As a child she can enchant, charm, and use all manner of bewitching spells.

As a young woman she can heal, and sometimes even resurrect.

As an old crone she casts new majicks and can answer any question posed to her, but only ever one question per phase.

Extra Info:

Child of Morn, as Mother of All, before whom gods tremble, and as Goddess of Dark, Quiet and Frightful One who has her meal amid the graves. She is exalted as the supreme goddess of time and space
