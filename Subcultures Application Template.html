Noble House Creation


For members who wish to add their own Noble House to this world they must submit this completed form to the Profile Applications forum in the Character Creation Centre. All types of nobles houses use this template, all races and cultures must use this template and submit it completed to the profile application forum. Please state in the topic description that it is a noble house application and which race or country it is for. Thank you.

PLEASE NOTE The Avalon Noble Houses and Royal Family Tree's that contain the most powerful and prominent houses MUST be taken into account when you make your own house. As they are currently under construction and few canon houses have been officially added at this point in time it will be very easy to get your noble house accepted.

You only have to complete the House Info if you wish to add your noble house to the History of Avalon. If there is a significant addition to the History of Avalon then please point it out and make sure you let a staff member know if a new profile contradicts your info. We are not perfect and forget some of the stuff we add, let alone the vast amounts members add.
