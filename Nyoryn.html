FREE LANDS


No one owns this land but it is maintained by the Magnate and his Marshall.

Dwarf Colonies


Several of these dwarf run colonies are scattered throughout the land and mountains of Nyoryn. Mostly used has haves for Dwarves travelling and trade centres, they also use the mountains to mine.

Elf Colonies


Both Forest Elves and Mountain Elves have colonies in Nyoryn. The Forest Elves are situated in the huge forests of Nyoryn, but few ever stay for long. As they are not in Sanctuary those who stay tend to Fade. It is well known as a place for tired elves to go, and leave this world...

Alatus Farms Provinces - Scattered throughout the free lands the Alatus run these area's as they run there own City State of Volatilis. Their laws apply in this area of Nyoryn. Mostly rural-agricultural, there are a few humans who cultivate the land alongside the Alatus who are assigned there.
Synopsis of Country/Region:

Nyoryn is a simple country, with kind, simple people. There are no major cities. The leader dwells in various stately homes and manages the country as Baronies. Each region has a baron that reports to the Magnate. Many colonies of other races are within the borders of Nyoryn, land delegated to them as a gesture of goodwill.

The People of Nyoryn:


The people of this country vary drastically, depending on their race, region and religion. There is nothing universal or absolute about Nyoryn. Its multicultural society breeds tolerance and acceptance though. It is very rare for someone to feel unwelcome or shunned as everybody is so different and there is much freedom of movement and trade and socialisation.

Geo-political Infrastructure:


Each Barony is run by its Baron (high noble) and they run the province like a state. The magnate is elected and can over-rule a Baron, especially if he is being tyrannical (which is rare).

The Magnate Herok Landil oversees the running of the country.

The Marshall Serg Seril runs the army, guards the borders.

Religion and Culture:


Nyoryn has many cultures and religions. Many different kinds of religions thrive in Nyoryn due to its neutral status. Clans, tribes, the principalities, (Djinn Worshippers, Merchant Guilds, Menageries, nobles, all races live in harmony alongside one another. All cultures and religions are welcome and thus far everyone has co-existed in peace.

Laws of the Land:


The laws depend on the Barony. Peace is maintained by the Marshalls Guards though, and it is peaceful.

Trade and Currency:


Trade is slow at the moment. Political disagreements with their neighbours along with the lack of ports means Nyoryn lacks trade and therefore many goods. It is a productive country though, with lots of land set aside for agriculture. Trade mainly takes place between the Baronies as different regions do tend t specialise in different products.
