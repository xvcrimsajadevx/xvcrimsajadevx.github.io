Overview of the Magus


The Magus are the close servants of Emperor Drakki of Eraosia and are all mind Sorcerers, otherwise known as Psionists. They were raised by him , they are his order and only he commands them.

There are always seven, and like their leader, Drakki they always wear a mask, a different animal depending on their rank; the ranks are as follows:

1. Draconian
2. Falcon
3. Wolf
4. Sea creature (some believe to be the design of a fish, but it is impossible to tell what it is specifically due to the design of the mask)
5. Wild feline (such creatures have become rare and so the name of the specific design is lost to most who have seen the mask)
6. Centaur (though not considered and animal by most, the Drakki has this as an insult)
7. Ram

Magus are selected by Drakki only and they serve him implicitly. As no one knows the true identity of these Magus it is hard to tell if the same individual is always behind the mask. However Drakki knows the true identity of them all and if he decides to he will dispose of them and replace them. Some sorcerer's are earmarked for future Magus service and replace the deceased Magus and some Magus may be assassinated and their killer is sometimes employed as the new Magus if Drakki desires it.

Their powers are all mind based even though they have to have previously trained in one of the schools of Lightsbane. All potential Magus and current Magus must have the Mind majick talent (often called Psionics). This is an Ether ability, and indeed one of the Talents. If they do not have this talent but are an exceptional individual or Drakki demands their service it has been known for a sorcerer to be 'granted' this ability upon ascension in the order of Magus. The higher the rank the more Mind Majicks and talents and psionic spells they have.

Psionics


Psionics is the study and/or practice of using the mind to induce telepathy, telekinesis and other workings of the outside world through the psyche. The term "psi" means both extrasensory perception and psychokinesis. The most widely utilized psionic ability is telepathy, closely followed by telekinesis and precognition.

The Magus also use this power to induce madness as a sort of overwhelming telepathy in the minds of those who Drakki wants to punish or who may be a threat to the emperor (or just because the Magus feel like it). Psionics is an advanced form of mind majicks and can only be used in this form by ether users.


Psionic Abilities may be categorized into three types:

First Person Psionics - I -


- This type of Psionic ability is personal, as in it is useful only to the user:

** Psycho-metabolic abilities ("mind over body")
o Resistance to Pain
o Personal Shielding - To resist assault or external injury
o Accelerated/Guided healing
o Lessening mental instabilities
** Extrasensory Perception ("third eye")
o Precognition (seeing the future)
o Postcognition/Retrocognition (seeing the past)
o Remote Viewing(seeing beyond one's own surroundings)
o Spiritual Perception (seeing beyond the planes of this reality)


Second Person Psionics - You -


- This type of Psionic ability can be interpersonal, as in useful or effectual against more than one person:

** Telepathy: Communication without the use of physical senses. ("mind speak")
o Thought Communications
+ Discourse Projection (saying conversation without mouth)
+ Discourse Reception (hearing conversation without ears)
o Image Projection (transmitting images into another's mind)
** Mental Persuasion ("brainwashing")
o Mind Control
o Mind Reading
o Memory Alteration
o Induced Hallucination
** Clairperception (perceiving the truth in an object or person similar to an extra sensory perception)
o Clairvoyance (perceiving the truth in an object or person through sight)
o Clairsentience (perceiving the truth in an object or person through feeling/touching)
o Clairaudience (perceiving the truth in an object or person through hearing/listening)
o Clairalience (perceiving the truth in an object or person through smelling)
o Claircognizance (perceiving the truth in an object or person through knowing)
o Clairgustance (perceiving the truth in an object or person through tasting)
** Therapeutic Uses ("mental healing")
o Lessening mental instability in another
o Third-party mediation of internal conflicts


Third Person Psionics - Them -


- This type of Psionic ability can be confrontational, as in useful or effectual against more than one aggressive person, or may affect target objects:

** Psychokinesis "mind over matter"
o Telekinesis (Manipulating/Creating kinetic Energy with the Mind)
o Pyrokinesis/Cryokinesis - Manipulating/Creating Heat Energy with the Mind [to burn or freeze targets
o Electrokinesis (Manipulating/Creating Electrical Energy with the Mind)


Magus tend to use the more attacking and forceful spells, such as mind control, memory alteration and induced hallucination rather then Clairperception.
