Azaliz


The Capital City, the centre of the Azaili Empire, and the location of the Zatu Palace. The Queen of the Azail is Shaecaer?, she is the daughter of Ahto and Yula.

Azaliz is completely submerged and can only be accessed via the two islands that mark the Gap of Ahto (which was made using the jewel of Orion and the Kraken by the King of the Azail, the much mourned King Ahto). The city itself is a catacomb of underwater tunnels and buildings, the depths of the reef, on the ocean floor is the temple of Azure, where only the King or Queen of the Azail can enter...
