Summoners


GROUP/GUILD/CLAN/TRIBE NAME: Summoners

RACE: Human, sub race of human.

COUNTRY/REGION: None specific

LIFESPAN: The Ether does not extend their life, they live as long as normal humes. The reason for this is their use of the Ether is burnt out and they do not/cannot summon the Eria so their life is not extended by these gods.

APPEARANCE:

Varied, depends on the individual.

PERSONALITY TRAITS:

As above.

HISTORY:

When the Eria changed humes into Quasi there was a natural evolution of humes born with the ability to summon creatures from the Shadow Realm. They were not able to use the Ether as Wizards and Mages but they were able to summon lesser Shadow Realm creatures.

MAGIC:

Summoning: the elder summoners are rumoured to know how to summon the Eria but have taken an oath not to. They believe the Quasi are committing heresy by calling upon the gods and that the summoning ability was not meant to prolong life or be used to call upon the Eria. It was only to be used to summon the Shadow Realm creatures. These summons are numerous and varied. The summoner is able to conjure creatures at will and the summon is able to stay in the Nether Realm? longer then most Eria. They are less powerful, smaller and easier to control.

EXTRA INFO:

Examples of creatures summoned are;

Shadow Panther
Shadow Hawk
Shadow Draconis

Basically any animal can be summoned in its shadow form and many unique creatures to the Shadow Realm summoned too. Players are free to create creatures their summoners can call upon but must state what their character's summoning capabilities are.

Creatures from the Shadow Realm have unique powers. Anything (within reason) is possible for these creatures as they are made from the Ether. A list of your summons abilities/magic must be included in your profile also. The summoned creatures cannot die, when they are injured they return to the Shadow Realm to rejuvenate. Some are corporeal some made of pure shadow, black mist, ethereal beings. It depends on the creature, the summoner and the experience, talent and strength of the summoner.
