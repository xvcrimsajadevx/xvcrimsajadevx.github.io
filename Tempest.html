Tempest


Tempest is the Origin, or Goddess, of the Air and creator of the Race of Dragons.

Appearance


Tempest is the wind, the clouds and the rain. She can appear as a beautiful maiden, or an ethereal spirit, shrouded in mist with a crown of raindrops and two small suns for eyes. Her voice is the whispering wind and the roaring thunder, her wrath the hurricane winds and the lightning bolts...

History


Tempest awakened with the first Light, blinded by the new born stars. The space between the worlds fascinated her, and saddened her. She wanted to be with the stars and shroud the new formed lands with her love. And so, she wrought her power and created the blue skies, and covered the worlds in clouds. Upset that she could not reach the stars, so far from her, she wept and her tears became the first rains. She toughed the land with her lightning fingers and whispered her love for the Light on the Winds. Shading Creation from the harsh heat of Pyra's golden spheres she wove air and light and made the clouds.

On the highest peaks of Titans mountains she hovered, looking over Creation, and felt alone. She was alone in the skies, the worlds were empty of life. So she was the first to make life. She drew from the air and formed Nado from it, giving him solid form, and wings, wings so he could fly with her in the heavens! He was part of her and roamed the skies watching the other races birth. But he was lonely, even the companionship of Tempest could not stop his longing for another of his kind, so Tempest made him a female, and called her Tiamat, daughter of Tempest and Mother of all dragons.

When the darkness cursed her children Tempest wept, great storms raged but Tempest still wept, flooding the lands with her tears.

For millennia she was nothing but the wind, being tossed to and fro, watching her children die, lost forever to the darkness. When Orion sung to her, calling her down from her clouds, she stopped crying. She promised to come when her child called and sealed her promise into the diamond that bore her name. Whoa to those that stand against her in the End, for the wrath of Tempest will rage until all that threaten her children is no more... when the Light is safe and the Darkness sealed. Only then will she stop to weep for them...

Majick


Tempest's powers can only be used by her races. Each spells strength depends upon the ability and knowledge of the caster. When the spells can be cast depends on whether or not the sky is visible when doing weather spells, or how much air is is a room if the caster is confined (the smaller the room, the less likely they are to be able to shatter a door with blasts of air). To generate air and wind is only for the most powerful casters. I.e one cannot cast the Wrath of Tempest if one is in an underground cavern. How these spells are cast also varies depending on the race of the caster. An Alatus may control the wind differently to a Dragon, and visa versa.


Spells include;

Air Blast


Destructive blast of air which can take the forms of a sphere, arrows or a spear.

Zephyr of Terah


Localised storm wherein there is rain, wind, thunder and lightening. Often contained within a bubble, this Zephyr is a powerful storm that is large enough to encapsulate a city, or a smaller area, depending on the power of the caster.

Whirlwinds of Fate


Multiple tornado's large and small summoned by the caster. Number of whirlwinds and size depends upon the strength of the caster and well as their degree of control.

Shield of Air


Solidifies the air into the shape of a shield. Often a blue hue glows to indicate where it as. Fastens itself to the forearm of the caster or floats and becomes a mobile barrier depending upon whether the user is in a melee or magical battle. Multiple shields can be cast by stronger individuals.

Symphony of Air


A melody that the caster sings to change the direction of the wind.

Blade of the Djinn


A lightning sword summoned to aid the caster. The forearm is wreathed in lightning, forks shaping the sword, sparks cutting the air.

Wrath of Tempest


Control of fierce thunder and lightning. Harnessing the powers of an already existing storm.

Whisper on the Wind


An aid to or heightened hearing. Uses the oscillations of the air to enhance the sounds waves of distant voices or events.

Constrict


Creates a prison of air that solidifies around the subject and stops them from moving, but allows breathing.

Air Rope


Used to bind as a real rope would. Hands, feet, or full body, can also be used to abseil, or do anything a normal rope would.

Stairway to Heaven


Steps made of air that solidifies to create access to places otherwise not reached (usually races of Tempests can fly so this spell would be used when they cannot access somewhere by flying or to give access to those who cannot fly.)

Dome of the Gods


Protective dome of solid air. Can be a personal barrier or a cities barrier. Completely encompasses the object of its protection. Is virtually impenetrable, and lasts as long as the Gods will it. Or depending upon the caster. :p

Dragons


Dragons are the primary race of Tempest. They have their own magic in addition to what is here as well as a rich history. Check out the magic section to see what spells they utilise.
