Weapons Masters of Toah



RACE: Only humans see the Djinn as gods, therefore only humans would worship Toah in this way. It is rare for members of other races to join this group, but it is not impossible.

COUNTRY/REGION: With temples all over the world his people tend to stick with him and inhabit the most simple of the temples, which unfortunately for those who wish to learn, are not on Avalon. Humans tend to get carried away with their worship of Toah and end up driving him away from their continent.

LIFESPAN: Depends on the weapons master.

APPEARANCE:

Bare chested, and mimicking their god's apparel, the men wear topless robes, are tanned from working in the sun, and muscular from working the forges.

PERSONALITY TRAITS:

Focused, dedicated and talented blacksmiths, the weapons masters are monk-like in their dedication to their craft. Those with magical abilities are akin to the alchemists on the Wizarding Isles.

HISTORY:

As a Djinn who does not ask for worship, he ignores the more elaborate edifices built for him. The smaller temples are more like worships where he teaches highly skilled Humans, Dwarves, Elves, whoever wishes to learn. For centuries the weapons of the Weapons Masters of Toah have been as eagerly sought after as the weapons he forges himself.

He will not teach anyone outside of his followers and will not make weapons for races of the Darkness. Only followers of the Light are allowed to learn and know the secrets of the Djinn's weapon making, his swords being the most sought after in history.

MAGIC:

Those who have magical ability have similar majicks to the Alchemists.
