Overview


COUNTRY/REGION: All over the Nether Realm?, but they tend to hang around their individual temples which are mainly in Avalon?.

LIFESPAN: Immortal

APPEARANCE: They all have individual appearances, but the common factors are their humanoid bodies and glowing eyes. These eyes are different colours depending on the Djinn, and their skins, height and number of limbs etc are varied, but they all appear close to human.

PERSONALITY TRAITS: It varies from Djinn to Djinn, but most have a superiority complex and think they are close to gods.

History


The Djinn are an immortal race made by the Ether. Some races of the Nether Realm? disrespect them by thinking of them as genies, but their status is more that of demi gods. They don't grant wishes per se.... more like, answer prayers. They are not bound to answering a humans desire either. Some take it as a serious duty, some use their followers to gain power in the Nether Realm?.

They are above humans and some of the other races, but below the Origins.

They are the embodiment of the Ether.

There are only two races that come from the Ether, the Djinn and the Eria. These races have in turn made races of their own.

Majick


Their power lies in their epithet, and is the reason they are worshipped by humans. Humans think of them as gods, and only the wisest and oldest humans (wizards) know their true nature.

The Djinn are wise and powerful, but are bound by their nature to humans, although not forced to grant humans blessings, some in fact twist the meaning of a desire and end up cursing the human. But they are bound and limited by their powers, which creates jealousy and rivalry between some of them.

Temples


If the location is unknown it can be assumed there may be more then one temple. Players are free to say where a temple is in this case. In most cases there are several temples dedicated to any one Djinn.
