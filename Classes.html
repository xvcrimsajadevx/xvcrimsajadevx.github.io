Classes

This page is under revision. Members are welcome to create their own classes. Assigning a class is optional.

Prestige Classes

Class can be a fundamental part of the identity and nature of a character. A character's capabilities, strengths, and weaknesses are largely defined by their chosen class. If a player does not wish to assign a class they can either leave the field blank or use an occupation instead.

Fighter Classes


Paladin


Paladin's are a Fighting Class. Lawful Good.

(NOTE: Paladins can be found anywhere and normally have a god they serve but not always. Dark Paladins are unique but not unheard of.)

Knight


Knights are a Fighting Class. Any alignment.

Barbarians


Barbarians are a Fighting Class and a sub race. Any alignment.

Assassin


Assassins are a fighting/rogue class. Any alignment.



Majick Classes


Majick classes are optional but normally applied. A trained majick user is someone who has attended formal training or had a mentor teach them. Basic or untrained users are classed as such because they are normally self taught. As always there are exceptions to the rule.


Wizard


Wizards are a trained majick class, always Light/Good. Use white majick. (There are neutral wizards but once a wizard crosses to darkness and becomes a minion then they are sorcerers by class as they use different magic.)

Sorcerer


Sorcerers are a trained majick class, and always Dark/Evil. Use black majick. (There are neutral sorcerers but once a sorcerer crosses to light then they are wizards by class as they use different magic.)

Shaman


Shamans are a trained majick class, can be any alignment.

Mage


Mages are a basic (or untrained) majick class. Any alignment.

Cleric


Clerics are a Trained Majick/Fighting Class. Lawful Good. Alignment ALWAYS Light as their powers come from the Light or from an Origin. Never ether users!

Summoner


Summoners are a Trained Majick User class.

Quasi


Quasi are a Trained Majick Class.

Priest/Priestess


Priests/Priestesses are a Trained Majick Class (although some have no magic and are merely worshippers. Not all gods imbue their high priests with magic.)

Sword Dancer


Sword Dancers are a battle majick class added by Lionheart.


Druids


Druids - such as the Druid's of Omri? - are a majick class that specialise in nature majick.


Other Classes


Merchant


Merchants are a Trade Class. Any alignment

Gypsy


Gypsy's are a Semi Trained Majick Class.

Rogue


Rogues are a Thief class. Any alignment.

Ranger


Rangers are a fighting class. Normally good/Light/Titan alignment.

Warrior Priest


Warrior Priests could worship any god/goddess and are a fighter/majick class. Can be any alignment and tend to have clerical powers to aid their combat with weaponry.



Occupations



Merchant


A class and occupation, merchants travel all over the world and sell their wares. A Peddler is a lower and less wealthy form of merchant. Many of the wealthiest merchants live in the Lacanta Ilse's and have great mansions and many servants.


Dancer


Employed or self employed.


Bard


Spinner of tales, actor, musician and sometimes enchanter. Sometimes considered a magical class as well as occupation as their music often has enchanting effects and the power of their voice can also be very persuasive.


Musician


Instruments in the Nether Realm are those similar to the ones found in any medieval society. the harp, flute, drums, chimes, etc.


Soldier


Normally attached to an army or militia of some sort.


Healer


Very basic abilities, not always magical. Some are herbalists, some have more complex powers that are innately magical.
