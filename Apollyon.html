Apollyon

Affinity: Death

Appearance:

Apollyon has red orbs suspended inside a dark cowl, no determinable face or features. The eyes glow fiercely when he is emotional. He is always arrayed in dark robes, with necromantic symbols adorning them. His voice is deep and strong, and quite melodic actually. He is can lure someone into a false sense of security with his tone, and strike when they least expect it.

Temples:

His temples are usually bare, made of black onyx stone and always located underground, his main temple of the Isles de Mortis being unique and the only exception. That is more like a fortress, a castle shrouded by mist and made of a dark greyish stone that seems to change its hue depending on sunlight.

Powers:

He has all the powers of necromancers plus infinitely more. As a Thyrza he has dominion over some of the Other Realms, the demon dimensions and particularly Oblivion. He is the Lord of the Dead and can steal souls and mark souls before the person has died. They become his slaves and are his creatures.

As lord of the dead he has no fear, but he can be cautious and suspicious of the other Thyrza. He does not take their power lightly, and as is the nature of the Djinn, he becomes rather jealous of their position and power and does everything he can to consolidate.
