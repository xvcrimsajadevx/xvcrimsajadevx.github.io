Obsidian Elves



Region: Forgotten Isles

Appearance:

They can change their appearance so they can look like other elves (the mimic ability), the only thing they cannot change is their eyes. Obsidian Elves have a trademark pinprick of a purple glow in the midnight pools of darkness. To the untrained eye they appear no different from other elves when they are in this guise. Whenever they leave the Forgotten Isles they always take on the natural camouflage, one that they have little control over. Only the highest of Obsidian Elves can control the way others see them.

Their natural appearance on the Forgotten Isles is a skin that appears to have a purple tint, their hair either jet black, silver, dark purple or dark green. Their nails black, their long limbs and slender necks make them enchanting even in their true forms. A dark beauty emanates from them, their features not reflecting their black hearts at all. Of course not all Obsidian Elves are evil, but when you are brought up in a culture dominated by Darkness, it is hard to be anything but the malcontent.


Personality:

Born evil, those that do turn to the light are few and far between. Malicious, sadistic, deceptive and cruel, the obsidian elves would kill their own kin to further their own reputation and power.

There is an innate hatred and jealousy for other alamia in the hearts of the obsidian. Even they they rejected (and for the most part continue to reject) their creator Titan, they still harbour a petty jealousy of those the origin now favours. They hate all races of Titan, and because of their alliance with the Dark Kindreds they also despise dragons.

An Obsidian elf has no loyalty to blood or clan, they continuously seek to improve their own status and dispose of anyone who gets between them and their goals. They are devious, untrustworthy and maligned,. They may fight under the banner of the Darkness and their armies are formidable, but their petty infighting makes it impossible fr them to be successful in keeping a foothold anywhere of consequence in the Nether Realm.

Their repressed need to want to be accepted by their former brethren has meant they have evolved a mimic ability. It is a natural talent fr blending in with the other alamia on Lenal?. Other Obsidian Elves can tell apart one of their own from a normal alamia even when their brother or sister is using this ability. They see them for some part as they truly are. The mimic ability does not stop at appearance alone. They can also change their vices and even mimic certain elven majicks. This is why the Obsidian's are able t infiltrate Lenal and are able to sustain a dangerous resistance upon that small continent.

As with all things there are opposites. There is a rebellion within the Obsidian Elf society also. Some Obsidian's choose to follow the Light and seek to be reunited with Titan. These rebels call themselves 'The Lost', and have quite different personality types/traits to their brethren. Meditative, and often suppressors of their sorcerer powers - adept in healing majicks and wielding weaponry; entire clans are thought to belong the The Lost, but because of the nature of this kind of resistence their numbers are a mystery.


History:


The wars and betrayals of these elves are many, most of which are recorded in their time-lines and historical texts. Yet, none mention the truth of their origins.

The sixth alamia were the last to awaken in the Alumi Grove?. They were the youngest elves, and their Elders awoke in shadow... the newly formed suns of the Nether Realm were already setting and the world was suffused in shadow and doubt. Already their brethren were gathered around the Rock of Ages and were conversing with their creator. The Obsidian elders did not approach Titan, and refused to learn the majicks their Origin wanted to teach them. They sought the shadows beyond the mountains and rejected the paths their brethren chose before them.

They were also the first to murder.

Cast out by Titan and stripped of their immortality the Obsidian Elves fled Lenal? and sought the Darkness their black hearts craved.

The place where these Elders first set foot upon the Dark Continent? is where the Onyx City? now stands. Here they were changed and given a new type of immortality. Their souls were bound to Oblivion and fused with the Ether. A twisted version of what they once were, still possessing the beauty of the elves but cursed with a tainted soul, they became an adopted race of the Darkness.

A complex and feudal society evolved upon the Forgotten Isles, the Dark Kindreds and the Obsidian Elves allying to create the most ferocious and terrible army of Darkness the worlds had ever known...

Magic:


These elves are very unique with their magic and very different from true elves. They have all the Shadow Powers, and a very good mastery of telekinisis. All Obsidian Elves are born with the ability to use the Ether, but cannot use Titan. They are elves in look and name only, cast off and cut of from Titan altogether. They are masters of Necromancy, whatever school of Sorcery they choose ALL Obsidian Elves are made to master the Death Majicks.

So their basic majicks are;

MIMIC
DEATH MAJICKS
SHADOW POWERS -


SORCERY

As they are born sorcerers they can also learn spells in all the schools of . Some Dark Elves even go to House Lightsbane to be trained, but most train on the Forgotten Isles as the Obsidians have their own Grand Masters in Sorcery. The Obsidian speciality is death magic, and many are fully trained necromancers - it is mandatory for all to have a few death majicks mastered, this is taught in infancy (increasing the chances of these elves to be malign and therefore fewer turn to the Light).
