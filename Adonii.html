Adonii

Affinity: Lightning

Appearance:

She has long hair, which is platinum blonde with bright yellow streaks zig zagging through it. Her eyes Are also glowing white, but like a cats eyes, except the yellow slash it a lightning bolt that flashes and pulses. Her robes are white and yellow also, and barely cover her curvaceous body with their thin, see through silks.

Temples:

Her temples vary as do her tastes. The only consistency about her or her temples are the three towers and spires included in their architecture.

Powers:

Adonii is a volatile Djinn and hell bent on power and her rivalry with Iri. She uses her lightnings to force people to worship her, destroying villages and even towns with it and making them bow down and acknowledge her as a goddess. She demands temples to be built to honour her. In disguise she appears as a beautiful noble, but as she still has a volatile temper sometimes ends up burning people to a crisp. Using her beauty and determination she tries to overpower Iri and his followers as well as gain ultimate power over humans.

Extra Info:

The sworn rival of Iri, Adonii commands her own worshippers. Often posing as a noble, and influencing the politics of Avalon, Adonii appears as a beautiful woman. Her temples are dotted all over Avalon, and those who follow her have a tattoo of a lightening bolt on their palm.
