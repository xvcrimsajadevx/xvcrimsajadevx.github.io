Ice Elves


Region: The Ice Continent?, Samain Isles?

Appearance:

White hair, pale skin, and albino eyes, the Water Elves are always dressed in fur cloaks and thick animal skins. Furred boots upon their feet, they are stockier then the average alamia, looking like the average build of a hume. Weighed down with clothes to keep themselves warm, they keep them distinct from the humes by adorning themselves with furs and fabrics that even the most brave of humes could not acquire. They also will were less when meeting humes to prove they are physically stronger and are not as effected by the climate as their humes counterparts. The Water Elves are still light on their feet and quick even when weighed down with these many layers of clothing. The pale features of these elves are harsher then their brothers and sisters on Lenal, more severe jaws and their ears bigger, their points sharper and more pronounced.

Ice elves are often confused with mountain elves by the ignorant. The main way to tell these alamia apart is by their eyes. All ice elves have albino eyes, this is a very distinct feature and there are no exceptions. Also, unlike the mountain elves, ice elves tend to have a somewhat cruel aura about them. Sly grins often adorn their faces, their body language reflecting their xenophobic nature. Often aggressive and stand offish, one would not be wise to approach them, even to ask for help, if they are taking this kind of stance.

The tall stature aids their tendency to loom and intimidate. Even though they are encumbered by thick clothing they are lithe. The ice elf females beauty is often hidden beneath the furs, their elven grace though is still able to shine through their tough exterior once they have shod the layers of clothing they hide beneath.

The men are the most rugged of elves. However, this is also hidden by a beauty of their own as well as graceful body language. Their voices carry a piercing and commanding tone, an eerie sound that punctuates the ice wastes in Ardra. They dominate with their stature and their tone, overwhelming and disrupting most people, elf or hume, carrying a dangerous edge in this tone that can become easily threatening even in the most innocent of conversations.


Personality:

Xenophobic and extremely rude. Don't bother asking these elves for help as they would not give it. Un-trusting and particularly isolated, not much is known about their society other then what the Elves on Lenal? reveal. The Water Elves are contentious with the other alamia and are usually the ones to create opposition. Their representatives on the council make it difficult by not voting unanimously. This often causes conflict politically as well as between the average elves.

The Water Elves of Ardra are as close to hostile as elves become. They can be cruel, and use mainly offensive magic's when they use Titan's magic at all. They take an instant dislike to anyone they do not know, even other ice elves. Ardrains have a deep hatred and resentment for the humes of the Glacius Regnum?, which they share with the Ice Giants of Kurn. This 'friendship' often leads to humes who are lost in the Ice Wastes being captured and tortured by the giants. The ice elves deny any affiliation with the giants.

Ice Elf nobility are the most pompous and arrogant of elves and their cruelty and malice cannot be matched. For a race of an origin these elves are very close to being cut off from Titan. The High Council of Elves suspects connections between the Obsidian Elves and the Ardrains but because of their limited access to the Sanctuary of Ardra, cannot investigate it.

Water Elves are sympathetic to their tainted brethren, and often voice how they wish they could bring back the Obsidian elves into the fold of Titan. This is considered heresy amongst most alamia, and even though only the Ice Elf nobility would voice these opinions, the hearts of minds of the average Ice Elf (whether in Ardra or Samain?) agrees with this dark and dangerous sentiment.


History:


0E - The Making of the Elves

- The Sundering of the Elves. Sub-races of elves were formed - these Alamia took on unique characteristics and traits depending on which part of nature they aligned themselves with. Some Alamia remain closer to Titan than others, such as the Mountain and Forest elves whilst other such as the Ice elves segregated themselves completely from Lenal.

1E-The Darkness curses the elves with the Fading; they are no longer truly immortal.

1E-The Darkness curses the elves with the Fading; they are no longer truly immortal.

-The Obsidian Elves are made, using Titans children. Cut off from Titan, these Dark Elves are born with the power to use the Ether and have no connection to Titan. They were severed from the moment the Darkness tainted them. Only the half breeds have fully escaped this fate.

-The Water Elves leave Lenal? and take their ships South, searching for colder regions. Some decide to go no further then the Samain Isles?, which are still a part of Elvenhome, the Continent of Lenal?

13E- The Water Elves establish Ardra as both a city and a region. The city is built upon the bedrock of Sanctuary - the only one known in the ice continent?.

327E- The Water Elves refuse emissaries from Lenal? access into Ardra. The Second Sundering takes place. They cut themselves off from Elvenhome for three hundred years.

531E - The High Council of Elves is formed. The first meeting was in the Alumi Grove?.

-Deleslaul Saleub and Oreguil Ulahia are chosen to travel to Vidonia to take their places on the Elven High Council as representatives of the Ice Elves.

-Ice Elves refuse to join the Alliance. Their representatives on the Elven Council resign.

3773E- The Water Elves of Lenal?, upset over the Summit, refuse to aid the search for Orion?. The Forest Elves decide to search the Ice Continent? themselves but before they can leave Lenal? are obstructed by the Ice Elves. The Battle of Naida ends with most of the Water Elves of Lenal? leaving, others isolating themselves in a secluded part of Lenal? - the Frozen Archipelago in the South of the continent. A bitter rift is established between the Water Elves and the Forest Elves.
