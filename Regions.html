Avalon


The Central Continent of Avalon is believed, by Humes, to be the centre of the world.

Here there are six Hume kingdoms, all varying in culture, climate and topography.

  • Eraosia - the land of Sorcerers, a land of darkness, and people of warmongers.
  • Sedana - a country that outlaws majick and believes in justice and altruism.
  • Nyoryn - a peaceful land made up of Race Colonies and Baronies.
  • Adenia - a beautiful country of rivers and strange cultures, the largest kingdom in Avalon and the most peaceful
  • Fenlar - a mountainous country that has echoes of Arthur's Camelot.
  • Teran A'ao - a desert land with mystical Shaman and mysterious denizens.
  • Province of Yaquinta - the Realm of Lady Aurora.



The Eastern Continent


The large land mass on the world map is known as the Eastern Continent.

  • Eramor - The only land some may deem as civilised.
  • Umikabi Mountains - Dragons dwell in the great mountain range, the largest and longest in the world.
  • The Wilds - Just that, wild. Few live here, and many beasts roam here.
  • Shalimdor and Kalimdor - the Calm and Quiet lands. Unicorns graze here and peaceful creatures abide here.
  • Great Desert - The Suna'arashi Empire resides within. It is believed that the fabled Temple of Kalami is also in these harsh lands, buried beneath the ever changing dunes of the hot desert.
  • Terra - where the Centaurs live and avoid all other races.





Lenal


Titan created Lenal so that his elves would ever be close to him. Not all of the "alamia" reside here, but many do.

  • Naida - a harbor where most other races trade with the Elves.
  • Vidonia - the Elven capital city.
  • Deltavyn - Port city
  • Vesta - dormant Volcano in the center of the island and Home to the Oracle of Fire.
  • Samain Islands - the ice elves domain on Lenal.
  • Amaris - Winged Elf city.
  • Dwindledwell - former forest elf region, now a taboo name, haunted and cursed.
  • The Rock of Ages - Titan's presence in the world embodied in stone.
  • The Alumi Grove - the grove where the elves first Awakened.



Azaili Empire


The underwater cities and abodes of the Azail are numerous and wonderful! Theirs is the biggest empire in the world and they control most of the waters. The deepest chasms of the oceans are theirs and they know all the secrets therein.

  • Azaliz - (city underwater), their capital
  • Urzaliz - (city above water)




Volatilis


The winged race of the Alatus reside in the floating city that flies through the skies of the Nether Realm. Only the Empress knows how this great land mass (almost 200 miles in width) flies.

The Southern Ice Shelf


  • Kurn - Home of Ice Giants
  • Ardra - Home of Ice Elves
  • Glacialis Regnum - Home of the Silvermyst Kindred and even Humes
  • Sephya - Land of Light and where the Hollow of the Ice Oracle can be found
  • Vallumglacia - The Great Ice Wall that surrounds Glacialis Regnum
  • The Tundra - The wilds where the nomads roam
  • Ice Wastes - The inhospitable wastes where fell beasts rule.

Only these races survive the Ice Wastes of the South. Similar in mass to our Antartica, the Ice Continent is bare except for a few pockets of civilisation and existence.
