Divination and Healing


Includes such feats as finding people and objects, contacting other realms, healing (greater and minor) and in some cases resurrection.

Minor

  • Exorcism – for example, exorcising/expelling a bewitchment.
  • Anti-venom – cures poison
  • Re-animate – cures petrifaction

Major

  • Restore – recovers a banished soul from Oblivion. Is a cure for the Oblivion spell.
  • Quintessence – releases tortured souls i.e. the souls of the undead or souls forcibly used in necromancy.
  • Awakening – resurrection.
  • Full Healing - major healing of an individual.

Alchemy


Specific to the creation or alteration of raw materials and imbuing them with magical properties.

Minor

  • Synthesize - Compounds and constructs the raw materials in preparation for other spells such as forge, imbue and amalgamate.
  • Amalgamate - blending different materials, like metals, jewels and magic into a fusion of Amal-misceo Items.

Major

  • Imbue - fuses objects which magical properties
  • Forge - forges magical weapons
  • Evanesce - Only this spell can destroy an alchemists work. Only the best Alchemists can perform it without the potion. Even with the potion, only an Alchemist will be taught these spells. The unfortunate thing is the Sorcerers Alchemists know this spell also...

Invokation


Support magic to call something to aid you. Object will disappear and then re-appear. Object needs to already exist. Static movement.

Minor

  • Wall - Creates a physical barrier using energy, and sometimes ripping trees and breaking rock to put a wall between the wizard and their enemy.
  • Vanishing/Removal – can make people/objects disappear from this dimension.

Major

  • Tempore – Manipulation of time.
  • Vortex – opening a gateway to the demon dimension.
  • Warping – opening a door to another location in the Nether Realm; only applies to the physical plane of existence.
  • Mind's Eye - Can invoke an object or even a person to appear, just by thinking about it, picturing it in the mind. Mind Wizards only.
  • Omen – opening a window to the past or the future to gain knowledge.

Illusion


Presenting a mirage over or no an object, as well as creating an illusion where nothing was present beforehand. The illusions can be any size and used for any purpose.

Minor

  • Ignis Fatuus - Hallucination: projecting a mass hallucination, or a simple one for a few in number. Effects the individual directly, each person seeing either the same thing or whatever the Illusionist puts in their individual minds.
  • Phantasm – creating an apparition, usually to deceive a single person. Always appears as a ghost. Not an actual spirit, just the illusion of one. The Illusionist can do with it as he/she pleases, using it for their own ends.
  • Daydream - The Illusionist can place daydreams into the head of their target. Often used as a distraction.

Major

  • Glamour – projecting a false image upon a person, including oneself. Disguising one's features and even one's voice.

Enchantment and Charms


Control/convince objects to change/move. More temporary control, but can have permanent effect/s.

Minor

  • Hypnosis - Enchanter can cast a spell that hypnotises the target, bringing them under his/her control.
  • Binding - A spell that uses energy to cluster and bind a target with an invisible rope. Can also be uses to bind magical powers. Depending on the Enchanter, the spell can last indefinitely or only for a brief amount of time, and of course anywhere in-between.
  • Levitation - This spells charms the object or person/people to fly.
  • Slumber- Induces the target into a deep sleep.
  • Translation of Text - This enables the person who the spell is cast upon to understand any text they read, no matter what the language.
  • Translation of Tongue - This enables the person who it is cast upon to audibly understand any language spoken to them.

Major

  • Possession - The Enchanter can seize control of the mind this spell is cast upon. As their own spirit possesses the body of the person they are controlling it leaves the wizards body vulnerable. If the person they are possessing is killed while they are in their mind, the wizard dies also. Similarly if the wizards body is killed, he is trapped in the body of the subject and will eventually have to relinquish control. The spirit fades and the wizard is no more.
  • Truth Spell - The person who this spell is cast upon will answer truthfully to anything asked of them by the spell caster, and the spell caster alone.
  • Freeze - suspends time for a particular object or person. Does not affect surroundings (i.e time flows normally for everyone/thing else) and is restricted to ONE person or object. Only very experienced and talented Enchanted can extend the field to more then one.
  • Amnesia – A spell that wipes the memory clean. It can be short term (wiping the memory clean of the last day or so) or specific to one thing (like an event) as well as a complete memory wipe of the entire past or long term events and prolonged forgetfulness.

Conjuration


The innovative school of wizardry, this involves creating. There can be nothing and then you bring it into existence.

Minor

  • Ward - A protection spell that guards against other attacking spells, especially when the wizard is un-awares. A Warding can be for anything, and often requires a alteration to the spell depending on circumstance.
  • Lightbringer - Conjures light, no matter where the wizard is.
  • Darkbringer - Conjures pitch blackness, no matter where the wizard is.

Major

  • Sword of Toah - Named after the Djinn of Swords, Toah, this magical sword levitates and fights for the wizard who conjured it, resistant to most elements and imbued with Light.
  • Light Arrows - Light arrows are conjured and shot at the target. Obliterates most Minions and is effective against the races of Darkness, particularly Vampyres.
  • Celestial Rage – summons comets and meteors to fall upon thine enemy.

Abjuration/alteration


Manipulate an objects state/form

Minor

  • Petrification – turns the target (animate or inanimate) to stone.
  • Shrivel - Shrinks the target. Certain area's of the body can be shrunk, like the head for instance. Or an object.

Major

  • Reverse Transformation - Reverses the transformation spell of a sorcerer or wizard.
  • Congeal - causes blood to clot to stop profuse bleeding. Aids with healing.
  • Replicate - Transforms the surrounding matter to replicate whatever the wizard wants to copy.
