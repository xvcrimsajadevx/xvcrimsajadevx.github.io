Merchants Guild

Race: All races

Country/Region: This guild has guild houses all over the Nether Realm. Lenal, Cendes, the Wizarding Isles, and of course, all over Avalon.

Lifespan: Depends on the member.

Appearance: Depends on the member and their race.

Personality Traits: Merchants are open and friendly on the whole, that is what this guild preaches, but always they will seek a bargain and to make money. So honesty is not always high on their agenda.

History:

The history of this guild has been a turbulent one. As the races have argued and wars have developed the links between the guild houses have been stretched. It is because of this guild that trade continues even in times of war, and most armies will allow their travel across borders as supplies need to get to their own troops as well. Recently it has begun trade with the Alatus in Volatilis, travelling there using griffins and some magic's available to them from the Wizarding contracts in the Wizarding Isles.

Magic:

Only magical items they trade really.
