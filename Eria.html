Overview


COUNTRY/REGION: The Shadow Realm

LIFESPAN: Immortal

APPEARANCE: Ethereal Spirits in the Shadow Realm, corporeal humanoids in the Nether Realm.

PERSONALITY TRAITS: Varies, depending on Eria. Basic traits are a longing to be a part of the Nether Realm and respect for the Quasi. They are the opposite of the Djinn on a whole. For every power hungry Djinn, there is a pacifist for an Eria, and vice versa.

History

[+]

The world was still young when the cataclysms started to tear it apart, when the Darkness fought the Light for control of existence. The powers raged, sundering the Universes, and splitting existence in two.

From this the Ether was created, from the sparks of good and evil, a neutral power was born. Ripples of this power resonated on all planes of existence, through all dimensions and Realms, but when those echoes touched the Shadow Realm, another race came into being...


The Shadow Realm is untouched by the Light and Darkness. It is cut off from the rest of existence, apart from one window, into the Nether Realm. When the Ether touched the Shadow Realm the Eria were born. Their existence was ignored by the Light, and dismissed as a mere by-product of the cataclysms, no part of its creation. The Darkness could not touch it, but that may not always be so, and time is merciless.

Made of Shadow and the Ether they were neither corporeal or without intelligence, but ethereal. They called themselves the Eria, the Forgotten Ones, and looked into the Nether Realm and watched the Light and the Origins wield their powers and create more races.

The world moved on, and these new races became more adept with their powers. The Eria looked on, some in envy, some in wonder, and desired to be a part of that world. Then, in an age not long past, a human breached the Shadow Realm using the Ether.

Reaching through the barrier between worlds he touched the Shadow Realm. This allowed the Eria to come through into the Nether Realm, using the wizard as a conduit. From that day on, the Eria were able to find those that could use the Ether, and come into the world through them. And thus summoners were made.

But the price that the humans paid was; they could only use the Ether through the Eria. The change made them wizards no more. By being touched by the Eria from the Shadow Realm, these potential wizards and sorcerers were cut off from the Ether.

Humans called them Summoners, because they could summon the Eria from the Shadow Realm, but they were known to the Eria as the Quasi.

It is a rare gift to be able to use the Ether, and even more rare to be touched by the Eria.

In the ancient times of humans, the Eria manifested themselves in the Nether Realm as gods to the Quasi. But not all Eria have the desire to touch the Nether Realm. Content with their existence and belief that the Shadow Plane is what sustains them, some Eria refused to claim the Quasi as a conduit to the Nether Realm.

Majick


The Eria use the Ether in the Shadow Realm as the Djinn do in the Nether Realm. They need a summoner to enter the Nether Realm, and once they are there cannot stay for long. The Quasi cannot support the Eria permanently as it takes a physical toll on them. When the Eria are corporeal they can wield the Ether depending on the Quasi they have used as a conduit power. Different Eria are adept in different spells.

For more information, look into the Art Of Summoning.

Shrines


On Aether is the Temple of Gates. This temple has an inner sanctum with a ring of archways. Each arch is a portal to one of the Eria shrines. The shrines can be found without going through the portals, but they are almost impossible to enter unless the Quasi passes through the portals. A shrine is a hallowed place, a place of significance, is it consecrated, special, the hallowed ground where the Eria first came into the world. For this reason, there is only one shrine per Eria.
