Human Merchants Guild

Race: Human only. They do have affiliations will many other races though.

Country/Region: Based in the Lacanta Isles, south of the Eastern Continent. Many ports and havens are in this hub of Merchant Trade.

Lifespan: Average human, three score and ten.

Appearance: It depends on the native country and wealth of the merchant. Most look like Sailors and travellers really.

Personality Traits:

Competitive, often dishonest, and ruthless, most members of this Guild built their success on trade and canny bartering. All levels of Merchant are included in this Guild and many members have their own Guild sub-divisions.

History:

The history of the Isles of Lacanta is entwined in the creation of this Guild. It is told that a merchant from Adenia gathered a merchant from each other country in Avalon and set off for somewhere to do trade with all. The ports and havens in Lacanta were built around where they formed the Guild, the Port now known as Ellinad.

Magic:

No magic, only magical items are traded.

Extra Info:

The Association of Peddlars are a sub-division of this Guild.
