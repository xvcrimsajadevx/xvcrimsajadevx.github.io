The Orb of Mantir


Legend...


All stories begin somewhere. Some are whispered on the wind, some written upon ancient tomes by the Gods themselves, and others are a grain of truth shrouded by the fallacies that time encouraged.

The legend of the Orb of Mantir is the grain of sand. It's truth lost in a desert of lies, both white and black, protected and fabricated for reasons even the 'Gods' themselves cannot fathom.

The whispers of this object of great power and mystery came from the now nomadic tribes of the Vigilia. The Orb has always been an object of worship for them, but its origins obscured by their secret nature and now silent tongues. The whispers grew louder though, but never did a Vigilia lose their tongue again to either confirm or deny. Some said the Orb was a key forged by the Gods. A key that opened the gates of paradise, gates that led to an afterlife that was an escape from the torments of Oblivion. Others said that in life though, one must not look upon the Orb. It was believed that the Gods made it from Light and Shadow, and that the Orb glows and pulsates, like an eye opening upon universes, it is wreathed in Darkness and shining an effulgent Light. If a living creature dares to touch the Orb then they shall be granted their most solemn wish. Their true hearts desire.


Powers of the Orb...


--Key and Seal (to Gates of Paradise)

--Twists the desires of the Unworthy and grants them a twisted version of their hearts desire (similar to how a genie grants wishes in our mythology)

--Raises the Dead - brings back an untainted soul from Oblivion.

--Reincarnates the Vigilia

--And finally - other powers depend on whom possesses the Orb (linked to the bearers desires, abilities and the shape of their heart - their innocence)

The Device


Legend


The Device is a weather device created by the Alatus. It is a combination of majick and Alatus technology and is the reason Volatilis can fly. It can control the weather and has been stolen and abused in the past, the result being devastating weather anomalies throughout the realm.

The Ny'eki are hell bent on stealing the Device and using it to defeat the Alatus, as the Alatus have used it to defeat them time and again.

Powers of The Device


The Device is a blend of machinery and majick given to the Alatus by their Djinn, Terah. The Device is connected to keeping the city of Volatilis floating as well as controlling the weather in and around the city.

Who Seeks The Device and Why...?


The Device is always entrusted to the Royal Family and according to the customs and laws of the Alatus, whoever is in possession of The Device (if not a member of the Royal Family) can petition the Council of Seats, (the High Seats of the Noble Houses) for the removal of the current Queen. Currently, in Volatilis, Seluci Ayperi is on one such mission to find the missing Device and return to Volatilis to take the throne from Tallina Enlil.

Also searching for The Device are the Ny'eki. They Ny'eki are a subrace of the Alatus, an anomaly cast from the city and looked down upon by the Alatus. For centuries the Ny'eki have sent wave upon wave against the Floating City in an attempt to regain control of their homeland. Every army that has flown against the city has been destroyed however, by the use of the Device and the user's ability to control the weather and strike down the warring Ny'eki before they could fly close enough to the city to do any damage. Never giving up, the Ny'eki continue to plot, scheme and search for The Device.
