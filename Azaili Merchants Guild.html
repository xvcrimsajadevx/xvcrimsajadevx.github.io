Azaili Merchants Guild

Race: Azail only.

Country/Region: Urzaliz

Lifespan: Same as Azail.

Appearance: Guild members have a ring that identifies them to other members. That is all that will distinguish them.

Personality Traits: Guild members are more outgoing then other Azail. They go on land more and have special permits from the Queen. They have a fondness for humans and like to trade in Aielin in particular. They are renowned for their fair trade and honesty. they make their money on their rare items.

History: The Azail have always traded, and only the Merchants were abroad during the Era of Silence. Their Guild House has always been in Urzaliz, the hub of Azaili Trade.

Magic: Azail magic. Their ships are built with magic and can survive the harshest storms. Azail also have the power to calms the sea's so their ships are always on time and very fast.
