Iduna

Appearance:

Idun appears as a young woman, but her face is always changing. She does not maintain the same features when in the Nether Realm for more then a few minutes. Sometimes she is blonde, brunette, red head, yellow eyes, green, blue and purple. Tall, short, slim and curvy, it all depends on her mood and whims.

Powers:

Iduna enchants with her music. When summoned she can cause several status effects with her songs...
