Religion in the Nether Realm depends on culture, alignment and of course personal choice. Many Realms in Avalon? have factions of Djinn worshippers, some worship the Eria, but there are Churches of the Light as well as Darker Religions who worship the Darkness.

Some hume cultures worship the Origins, and the Origin Races tend to worship their creator, their Origin. They may have different names for the Origin, but they do for the most part worship their creator.

Any of the Primal Powers can have a religion or religions attached to it. Members are free to create their own religions and their own gods, but if the god does not already exist then it cannot be added to the Pantheon. You may have another name but it must be the religion's name for an existing deity or powerful individual.

Clerics


Clerics are normally priests or priestesses who worship various deities and get their majick from their god/goddess. This can be one of the Origins, a Djinn or powers and religion relating to any of the Primal Powers. For more information on clerics see the Clerics article.
