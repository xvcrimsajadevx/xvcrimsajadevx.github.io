Tyra

Appearance:

A young man with jet black, long hair and black armour. He wields two swords, Oni and Quem. They are scimitar, one red, one black. His eyes are black with red pupils and his skin is as pale as death.

Powers:

He can cause fury amongst foes. His battle cry causes men to tremble in fear and sometimes burst their hearts. And his fury on the battlefield makes the rivers runs with blood. He is the personification of War. He can also get out of control and the longer he stays in the Nether Realm the more damage he inflicts, sometimes on friend as well as foe.

Extra Info:

Tyra is the brother of Elysia and appears as an ancient warrior. He considers himself a god of war, and in a battle sweeps the enemy before him. Legend says that Tyra is the shadow of the Djinn Morrigan.
