Urzaliz


The Azaili outpost located north of Aether? and off the north eastern coast of Avalon? in the Orira Ocean. Urzaliz is mainly above the surface of the waves, a great city that spreads for miles. It has some underground areas that are only occupied by the Azail, but above sea level its a metropolis. Many races trade with the Azail here as it is the only city where the Azail let other races roam freely.

Ur - means above water, and zaliz - means city in ancient Azail. Az - means below the water.
