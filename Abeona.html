Abeona

Appearance:

Abeona is purple skinned and has tubular strands of purple neon for her hair. Her eyes glow a brilliant white and she covers herself from neck to foot in a swirling black misty robe.

Powers:

Abeona can create gateways between worlds, and warp gates to other areas of the Nether Realm. She was the first Eria to breach the Shadow Realm and use a human magic user as a conduit.
