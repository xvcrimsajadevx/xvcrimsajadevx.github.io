Overview


Location: Flying over the Nether Realm

Major towns, cities or landmarks: Volatilis is a City State, it is the size of a small region, its sectors supporting thousands of people in its 10-100 square miles (depending on the sector)

Capital: Volatilis


Geo-political Infrastructure


The Royal Blood of the Alatus has the crown and currently House Enlil occupies the Throne of the Four Winds. Tallina Enlil is the Empress but she does not have complete power. She cannot do anything without a majority vote from the Heads of Houses. These leaders of the higher nobles are the Senate and have a lot of say in the governing of Volatilis.


Religion and Culture


The Alatus worship Tempest and Terah, their Creator and their gods. The Temple of Terah lies on the outskirts of the city, a lone floating isle connected to the palace via a narrow bridge. All Alatus worship Terah and pay their tributes to the temple. The priestesses of Terah are rarely seen outside of the temple.


Laws of the Land


  • No Outsider is allowed into the City without permission or without a guard.
  • No trade is done with Outsiders other then on the docks and parts of the Trade Quarter.
  • No commoner is allowed in the Imperial Sector unless they work for a noble.
  • At all times distinction of Rank must be adhered to and emphasized.
  • No Gambling.
  • No half blooded children.



Trade and Currency


The currency of Volatilis is coin. Gold, silver and bronze royal marks pass the hands of its denizens, along with jewels.

Trade between other races is limited. Those who can reach Volatilis find trade difficult unless it is a rare item and something the Alatus like. They have their own plantations, both on the floating city and on the land. Alatus Merchants are generally the ones who trade in the city, as they barter on the land and retrieve their goods from their mines, farms and market towns.

City Sectors


Residential Sector and Perimeter Farms


Volatilis is a city state, but has a fifty mile perimeter of green lands/plains. It is here many commoners live outside of the city walls, and beyond the slums, and many tend the farms which are the palace farms.

Slums


The poor Alatus live in the hovels under the main streets and on the outskirts of the City. This area is not scene by visitors and the nobles barely know of its existence, but exist is does.

Trade Quarter


The only area of the City the races of the Nether Realm can mingle. This is a huge area, but small compared to the rest of the city. It is a bustling metropolis. The docks are also located here.

Social Quarter


Here many of Volatilis normal inhabitants live and ply their trades. Theatres, sports arena's, taverns and brothels are found here.

Imperial Quarter


This is the high blooded part of the city. Huge mansions and smaller palaces line the streets, and the lords and ladies of the Alatus Nobility parade their wealth.

Palace of the Four Winds


Here be the wind throne of the Alatus. Only nobles and servants can enter the palace.
