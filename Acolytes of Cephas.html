Acolytes of Cephas



RACE: Normally Human, but some elves, particularly mountain elves, have been known to be among their numbers.

COUNTRY/REGION: The Umikabi Mountains

LIFESPAN: Depends on the human, or race.

APPEARANCE:

Normally dressed in such a way that they are camouflaged so they blend in with the mountainous region they live in. They even go so far as painting their skin to blend in as they believe in being one with the mountain.

PERSONALITY TRAITS:

Because of the nature of this cult very little is known about its followers. Some believe that they sacrifice themselves to the Mountain Djinn by throwing themselves from the heights of the temple, but this is all conjecture. The reason such stories are told is because the members of the cult simple...disappear.

HISTORY:

The Temples of Cephas of are located on every continent, one being made on each continent by the Mountain Elves to honour her. Her human followers make the pilgrimage to the Eastern Continent? as this is where they believe she resides.

MAGIC:

Not known.
