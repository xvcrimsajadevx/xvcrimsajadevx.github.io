Titan


Titan is the Origin?, or God, of the Earth and the creator of many races such as the Elves, Unicorns? and Centaurs

Appearance


Titan appears as his element. Rarely coming forth into All Worlds, but existing in the Heart of Creation, when he does manifest himself in the Nether Realm it can be as a statue, as a mountain giant, he can be made of stone, or trees, or mud. Believe when I say you would know Titan if you saw him, his voice makes the earth quake and his eyes glow with the Light.

History


The Heavens were filled with Light, the firmament sparkling with new born stars. The worlds of Light full of Light, but nothing else. From this nothing Titan awakened, blinking in the new light, the 'day' he took the nothing and made the Rock of Ages, the first lands, the first mountains, the first beaches. Trees and flowers and grass and fields, Titan wove his powers and gave form to Creation.

The Origin? of Earth laboured tirelessly, weaving his powers through All Worlds, moving mountains and shaping continents. Finally, when all the work was done, he made life.

Elves woke up in the forests, on the mountains, in the groves and on the beaches. His first Children blinked as he had done, in the light of the suns of the Nether Realm. Other races he made, but none so wonderful as his elves. They were the First, and his immortal race, at least until the Darkness stole their Light.

Lenal?, being the first continent he made, and the Birthplace of the Elves, became their Haven. The Fading took their Light from Existence, but he made Lenal? their Sanctuary, protecting them as well as he could from the curse the Darkness had laid upon them.

But another fate befell his Elves. The Darkness used his Earth Magics to create a twisted race, a dark race. Cutting them off made him weep, but they were lost before the first of them had breathed. He retreated into the Heart of All Worlds, leaving the Nether Realm to its fate. Until he was summoned by the Light, by his Child, his Orion?, summoned back to the First World to fight for his children and the Light. To fight for Nature, for Earth, for Creation....

Majick


Titan's powers can only be used by his races. Each spells strength depends upon the ability and knowledge of the caster. When the spells can be cast depends on what natural substances are available to the caster. I.e one cannot summon a sentinel if one is in the middle of the ocean. How these spells are cast also varies depending on the race of the caster. An elf? may heal differently to a Centaur?, and visa versa.


Heal


This is a primary spell of any race of Titan. The casting of it varies from race to race but the basic spell uses the earth to replenish and resuscitate the target of the caster, whether it be self or another. Whereas Nature's sacrifice drains the energy from a plant or tree to replenish lost energy a healing spell channels the life force of Nature itself and Titan to heal. Healing spells take more time and more energy from the healer. Generally used to heal others - this spell it difficult to perform on oneself and requires powerful casters to do so.

Petrify


Turns an inanimate or animate object/being to stone.

Shield of Rock


Skin toughens to various consistencies of rock depending on the caster.

Stone Marionette


Manipulation of surrounding rocks which then mimic the movements of the casters arms.

Rock Stronghold


Manipulation of surrounding rocks (mainly boulders) to form a wall.

Sentinel


Causes either an already existing statue (or creates a new one) to spring to life and protect the caster. Purely a defensive spell.

Rock Leap


The caster can walk into a wall and exit at whichever location they please as long as it is a part of the body of the rock they originally entered. Powerful casters only. (note: in order to enter the rock the caster must first sense what is widely known as the 'soft spots' on the rock walls. These are magically weak area's which are actually entrances to internal passageways that only masters of Earth Magic's can sense.)

Earth Shatter


Create earthquake(s) of various strengths and sizes.

Earth Breaker


Shatter rock into countless smaller pieces which are then used as projectiles.

Avalanche of Stone


Disrupt large area's of rock to cause a landslide. Stronger casters can even control the landslide as well as start it.

Earth Waves


Disrupt rocks on flat lands to create wvaes with them. Very destructive.

Vine Snap


Control of vines.

Tree Puppets


Bring tree to life which then mimics the casters movements. Stronger casters can animate multiple trees.

Shield of Bark


Tree's bend to shield caster.

Soil of Force


Manipulate earth into compact blocks which are then used offensively (shapes vary but the most popular one is a fist that is used to knock down walls and enemies.)

Avalanche of Soil


Disrupt earth which then undulates.

Whirlwind Earth


Disrupt earth to cause cyclones of soil. Drains a lot of energy from the caster.

Earth Immersion


Earth breaks and scatters so the caster can fall downwards, hiding underground in the sub terrain.

Soil Suicide


Manipulate small mounds of earth to form blocks that act as temporary shields.

Possession Spells


  • press ear to ground to hear the footsteps of any being within radius.
  • touch the ground to feel the presence of any being within radius.
  • touch a tree and possess it to either;
    • sense the presence of someone or something passing near another tree in the forest.
    • animate the tree and fill it with the purpose of protecting the caster. Once the caster has taken possession of the tree they can then move freely. The spell breaks once the caster is safe or the tree is destroyed.

Steps of Nature


Branches/leaves/vines move the provide a path or a way across otherwise impassable areas.

Blend


Can become camouflage and blend in with the surroundings.

Nature's Sacrifice


Drain any foliage of its life energy to replenish own. FORBIDDEN to be used unless near death. This spell is unacceptable to Titan is used to merely strengthen an already healthy person or being. This spell is always used as a last resort when the healer does not have the energy to heal themselves. An easy spell, and a quick one.


Elves


Titan created the Elves, and to them he gave his gifts of magic. Over time each alamia came to favour various spells and so taught each new generation to master these spells first and foremost. Each alamia chose five spells to base their magic teachings on. These spells have evolved as the Elves have evolved and can be found on the Elves page.
